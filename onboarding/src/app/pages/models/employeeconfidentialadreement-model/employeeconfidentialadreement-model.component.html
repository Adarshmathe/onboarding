During the course of such employment the employee will be susceptible to and gain access to certain information, the confidentiality of which ZFL desires to protect. Such Confidential Information includes all information, data or drawings, reports, memoranda, computer software works (such as programs, codes, manuals, print -outs, algorithms, and the like whether or not stored in any medium or system), correspondence, business records or other writing, whether or not patentable or copyrightable, relating to or in any way connected with ZFL, its affiliates, the properties or the assets, liabilities, business, operations, shareholdings, products, processes, activities or affairs of ZFL or its Affiliates, provided, directly or indirectly, by ZFL, its Affiliates or their Representatives to the employee, whether written, oral or stored in electronic or other form or medium, whether of a technical, engineering, financial, commercial, operational, economic or other nature and including but not limited to analyses, compilations, studies or other documents or records which contain, or otherwise reflect or are generated from such information, provided, however, that “Confidential Information” shall not include information which the Employee can prove that: (a) prior to the disclosure was already in his/ her possession; (b) prior to or subsequent to the disclosure was obtained by such Employee from a third party who is not in violation of any obligation of confidentiality or non-disclosure in making such disclosure; (c) prior to the disclosure was in the public domain; (d) subsequent to the disclosure was in the public domain other than by or through a breach of this Agreement by such Employee receiving information by virtue of this Agreement; or (e) is independently developed by such Employee without reference to Confidential Information (f) such information, that is mandated to be provided under any governing law, statute or by virtue of a demand from any governmental, judicial or statutory authority.

The Parties wish to agree on the terms and conditions to govern the confidentiality of such information:

Now therefore, in consideration of the employment by ZFL of the Employee and the compensation paid to Employee for such employment, the Employee hereby agree as follows:

  1. That from the date of his employment, till the date of him ceasing to be in employment of ZFL, the Employee shall work for ZFL and use his/her best efforts to further the interests of ZFL. Employee shall contribute ideas, inventions, information & improvements useful to ZFL and use his/her best abilities in performing such duties as he/she is directed to perform, from time to time, by ZFL.
  2. That from the date of his employment, till the date of him ceasing to be in employment of ZFL, and thereafter, except in the performance of his duties as an employee of ZFL, or as otherwise consented to in writing by ZFL, the Employee shall not:
    1. At any time or in any manner, make or cause to be made any copies, facsimiles, or other reproductions of the Confidential Information belonging to or in the possession of ZFL, or which may be produced or created by, or come into the possession of, Employee in the course of his employment.
    2. Remove any Confidential Information from the premises of ZFL, or fail or refuse to surrender the same to ZFL (notwithstanding the failure of ZFL to make demands for the same) immediately upon the termination of Employee’s employment or at any time prior thereto upon the request of ZFL.
    3. During or after his employment, directly or indirectly, disclose to or use for the benefit of any other person or concern, any Confidential Information or unpublished knowledge or information, in whole or in part, that he acquired during his employment regarding the present or future products, services, formulations, processes, designs, know–how, customers, suppliers, computer software works, business policies, methods of operation, marketing or pricing of ZFL or any present or future affiliate of ZFL.
  3. Except for discoveries, ideas and inventions which have neither a direct nor indirect relation to ZFL’s present or future business or which are expressly excluded in writing by ZFL from the provisions of this Agreement, Employee shall promptly disclose to ZFL, and to no one else, and hereby assigns to ZFL, all improvements, discoveries, ideas, inventions and copyrightable subject matter made or conceived by Employee, either alone or in conjunction with others, while in the employment of ZFL (whether or not patentable, whether or not made or conceived at the request or upon the suggestion of ZFL or resulting from any work done in the course of employment hereunder, and whether or not made or conceived during or out of the usual hours of employment or in or out of the premises of ZFL), all of which shall be the sole and exclusive property of ZFL. Employee shall, upon the request of ZFL, assist ZFL in obtaining the grant of patents, copyright registrations, and the like, and in enforcing and protecting the title thereto in India and / or in such foreign countries as may be designated by ZFL covering such improvements, discoveries, ideas, inventions and copyrightable subject matter. In connection therewith, Employee shall execute such applications, statements and other documents, and furnish such information and data (including but not limited to, the giving of testimony) as ZFL may from time to time request during or after the term of Employee’s employment. All expenses incurred in applying for, obtaining and enforcing such patent or copyright protection shall be borne by ZFL.
  4. Employee hereby acknowledges that, Employee has not, prior to his employment by ZFL, made any unpatented inventions which are to be excluded from the terms of this Agreement.
  5. This Agreement sets forth the entire understanding between ZFL and Employee concerning the subject matter hereof and supersedes all prior understandings or agreements. This agreement may not be modified, in any respect, except by an instrument in writing duly executed by ZFL & Employee.
  6. The waiver, by either party hereto, of any breach of any provision of this Agreement shall not be construed as, or constitute, a continuing waiver or a waiver of any other breach of any provision of this Agreement.
  7. This Agreement shall:
    1. Be binding upon the heirs, executors, administrators, successors and assigns of Employee & ZFL.
    2. Continue after the termination of Employee’s employment
    3. Inure to the benefit of and be enforceable by actions at law or in equity by ZFL & its successors and assigns.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the parties hereto have duly executed this Agreement as of the date first above written.
